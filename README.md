# Tutorial.HuntingTheManticore

Simple console program that emulates a scenario of a fortress in this case being called the manticore attacking a city, player1 will choose where to place the city from a range of 1 to 100 it is then player2 role to guess
the number and the command prompt will display wether player2 is lower or higher than the result when player2 guesses correctly it will deal damage depending on the round number to the manticore whereas every round that passes
the city will lose life whenever the city or the manticore hits 0 health the program will post a result and end.
